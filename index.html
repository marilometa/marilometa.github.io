01 / Practice

A practice built for the gap between vision and adoption.

Most Salesforce engagements fail at the seams: discovery vs. delivery, sponsor vs. user, architecture vs. operations. I work along those seams.

A: Architecture

CRM architecture & automation that holds up under scrutiny.

Data models, sharing & visibility, declarative automation (Flow Builder, validation rules, App Builder), and integration design aligned with the Salesforce Well-Architected Framework.

B: Strategy

Discovery, process design & value roadmaps.

Executive workshops, future-state agentic visions, no-code POCs that prove value in 4–6 weeks, and multi-year roadmaps that AEs and CFOs both can defend.

C: Adoption

Change, enablement & long-term account health.

Role-based enablement, release communications, executive briefings, and governance models that hold post-go-live: measured in sustained feature usage, not handoff signatures.
